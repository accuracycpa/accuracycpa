We Strive for Accuracy

Accuracy CPA, LLC, is a Certified Public Accounting firm serving the Southwest Missouri area since 2001, located at 1332 W Walnut St, Springfield, MO 65806. Clay Phillips, the Accounting Director and owner, is a member of the Missouri Society of Certified Public Accountants. We are invested in the betterment and wellbeing of the people in our communities and we are actively involved with several groups such as the local area Chambers of Commerce, local PTA’s, the LEAP Program and other community outreaches. We strive to help our community. We strive to support. We strive for perfection. We strive for Accuracy.

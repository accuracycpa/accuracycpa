About Accuracy

Accuracy CPA has been serving the Greater Springfield area since early 2001. We seek to help our clients achieve success through many years of expertise and learning. Our professional staff provide a broad base of technical expertise to clients in a variety of industries, while also being friendly and community oriented. Accuracy seeks to not only better individuals and businesses, but also to better our community through service and involvement.
